About The Project

A dual-purpose platform dedicated to environmental awareness and community-driven action.

System Overview

The AQI Monitoring and Community Event Management System is designed to bridge the gap between environmental data and actionable community efforts.

By integrating real-time Air Quality Index (AQI) data via the WAQI API, the system empowers users to make informed decisions about their health. Simultaneously, the platform hosts an event management ecosystem where volunteers can register for eco-friendly initiatives, track their contribution hours, and make a tangible impact on their local environment.
